  • Air Travel: The Dane County Regional Airport (MSN) is located roughly five miles from downtown Madison. A taxi from the airport should cost roughly $20.

    It is also possible to fly into either International Airport (ORD) in Chicago, IL or into General Mitchell International Airport (MKE) in Milwaukee, WI and then take a bus to Madison. From O'Hare one can take the Van Galder bus, and from Mitchell one can take the Badger Bus. Both of these have stops at the Memorial Union on UW's campus.

  • Campus Travel: Conference activities will all take place in Van Vleck Hall located at the top of Bascom Hill. From the hotel it is a short walk to campus. (Those wishing to avoid walking up Bascom Hill can should consider going down university and through the Botany Gardens.) A campus map can be found here.
