Applied Kinetic Theory Workshop for Junior Researchers at Wisconsin

University of Wisconsin-Madison,
April 19-21, 2019.
Organizers: Qin Li and Kit Newton.

We aim to bring young researchers working in kinetic theory and kinetic applications together to exchange ideas and facilitate collaborations. The focus is on mathematical analysis and computation in various kinds of physical sciences, such as neutron transport, radiation, plasma physics, fluid mechanics and medical imaging. This workshop is possible thanks to the support of National Science Foundation and the UW Math Department.
